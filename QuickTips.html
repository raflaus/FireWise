Quick Tips for Fire Prevention

storage

  • Don't store a battery fully charged for an extended period
  • Make sure devices are UL certified
  • Avoid overcharging your devices
  • Keep batteries away from direct sunlight and heat sources
  • Inspect batteries for any visible damage before use
