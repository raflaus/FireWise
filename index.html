Lithium-ion batteries have become an integral part of our everyday lives, powering devices like smartphones, laptops, and electric vehicles. However, it is crucial to educate ourselves about their proper use, storage, and disposal to ensure safety. FireWise is dedicated to providing valuable resources and guidance on lithium-ion battery safety, helping you prevent potential hazards and make informed decisions. Together, we can create a safer environment and extend the lifespan of our devices.

Quick Tips for Fire Prevention

  • Don't store a battery fully charged for an extended period
  • Make sure devices are UL certified
  • Avoid overcharging your devices
  • Keep batteries away from direct sunlight and heat sources
  • Inspect batteries for any visible damage before use
